This is a Inha University energy usage prediction model. Inha university energy usage data is provided by Inha University. The modified version of data is in "data" folder. This is a classified information, so please do not use it for other purposes.

The electricity usage prediction used Holt Winters method to predict the next 10 months of electricity use. The difference between prediction and real data may be bigger than expected due to lack of data provided.

The water and gas usage prediction used ARIMA model to predict the next 10 months of water and gas usage.
